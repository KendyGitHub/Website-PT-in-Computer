About Concepcion Holy Cross College

Concepcion Holy Cross College (CHCC), Inc. is a private, Catholic school in Concepcion Tarlac. It was founded by Pablo L. Tioseco, Sr. and his daughter, Dr. Raquel T. Sta. Ines, who wanted to provide students the opportunity to earn their degrees without having to travel far out of Concepcion. The college opened in 1995 with courses in Teacher Education, Computer Science, and Business Administration, including Secretarial courses. Four years later, the preschool and elementary departments were established.
